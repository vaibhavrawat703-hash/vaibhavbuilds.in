ABOUT

Product manager for systems that pick, pack, and move.

I'm Vaibhav — a Product Manager at ElasticRun, working on the SwiftER fulfilment platform. My work spans warehouse management systems, reverse pickup operations, client SOPs, and Andromeda, a greenfield AI-first customer platform.

I care about the gap between how ops actually run on the ground and how systems are designed to support them — and I spend most of my time closing that gap, one requirement, SOP, and workflow at a time.

How I like to work

I document as I build — SOPs, flowcharts, requirement trackers — because clarity on paper is what makes ops teams, tech teams, and clients actually move in the same direction. I'd rather ship something plain and correct than something clever and ambiguous.
